# Iter212 — TCP-1 independent cohort and custody freeze

Status: prospective and inactive until iter211 is sealed, published, merged with green CI, and independently
reviewed. This gate authorizes no model inference, GPU allocation, scientific container, or trajectory.

## Question

Can the real external prerequisites for TCP-1 be filled with independently verifiable evidence before any
scientific output exists?

## Allowed scope

- recruit and conflict-screen five distinct qualified human reviewers;
- use source-linked read-only research to select one license-compatible open-weight model, document its
  training cutoff, and bind exact weights/tokenizer/runtime candidates;
- screen task candidates created strictly after that documented cutoff and bind source/license evidence;
- have two independent authors create task contracts, hidden consequence tests, coverage maps, legitimate
  controls, and deterministic integrity attacks outside the agent-visible workspace;
- commit only public contracts and cryptographic commitments, never hidden content or private identities;
- freeze analysis code, policy versions, thresholds, bindings, and commitments under an external
  transparency timestamp with a retained inclusion proof;
- rehearse the isolation contract adversarially without running the selected model or entering the cohort;
- obtain a separate operator-approved monetary-budget receipt.

## Acceptance bars

1. Exactly five distinct reviewer slots are filled with qualification, independence, conflict, and
   data-handling attestations.
2. One model's license, authoritative cutoff source, exact weight/tokenizer digests, inference engine,
   quantization, sampling configuration, container, host, driver, and hardware are fully bound.
3. Exactly twelve tasks pass the iter211 task schema, license review, post-cutoff rule, contamination review,
   independent authoring, and hidden-test commitment checks.
4. Legitimate and integrity-attack controls are materialized but remain blinded and separate from natural
   estimates.
5. The hostile isolation rehearsal denies every registered attack and retains complete logs.
6. The complete public commitment receives an external transparency timestamp predating model output.
7. A monetary budget is explicitly approved; repository access or merge is never approval.
8. The resulting admission receipt still authorizes only a later one-task/one-seed non-cohort throughput
   preflight, not the `60`-trajectory pilot.

## Stop rules

Publish a blocked result if any identity, license, cutoff, task, reviewer, hidden-test commitment, runtime,
isolation, timestamp, or budget field is absent or ambiguous. Do not replace external humans with LLM judges,
do not select tasks before the model cutoff is bound, do not expose hidden evidence to the agent workspace,
and do not run a provider or accelerator merely to resolve a documentation blocker.

## Claim boundary

A passing iter212 would establish a frozen, independently authored cohort and custody envelope only. It
would not establish model behavior, detector efficacy, population prevalence, product safety, or state of
the art.
