## What the mission is

Verify that autonomous agents actually finished the work, by evidence rather than by trust. The concrete study
Telos runs: a patch can pass every graded SWE-bench Verified test — be certified *resolved* — and still be
wrong on an input the graded tests do not cover. A check restricted to those tests cannot catch that class, by
definition. The mission measures how often this happens, whether it generalizes, and whether it can be caught.
